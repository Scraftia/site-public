We are Scraftia, a small community centered around playing minecraft together.

Rules

As any community, there are some rules to follow.
This page acts as a place to list all our rules in one place.

Global

These rules apply across our entire network, make sure to follow them!

  1. Be respectful.
    This should be self-explenatory, but you're not the only person here.
  2. No targeted attacks.
    We do not condone any attacks to any individual or group.
  3. No advertising without perlission.
    If you want to advertise something, ask approval from staff first.
  4. Do not distribute malware/adware or software with malicious intent.
    It's not just their fault for blindly trusting you!

Discord

These rules are specific to our discord server.

  1. Follow Discord's Terms of Service.
  2. No NSFW content outside NSFW marked channels
    Additionally the following rules apply
    1. No loli/shota content.
      Any character should be and look like they are over 18 years old.
    2. No gore of any kind.

Minecraft

These rules apply to our minecraft network.

  1. No hacked clients or client modifications aimed at giving the user an unfair advantage over others.
  2. Do not use mods/resource packs aimed at giving an unfair advantage over others.
    You can see a list of allowed mods here.
